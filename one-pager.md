## What We Do

AI Agents autonomously operate physical gyms. A facial-recognition turnstile converts every entry into unforgeable proof of real human activity. That proof belongs to the person who earned it — DID-attested, on-chain notarized, their digital asset.

**We're building the verification layer for human physical behavior. TCP/IP for behavioral data.**

## The Problem

- Fitness behavior data is worth billions. Platforms capture it. Sell it. The person who generated it gets nothing.
- Current data is unverifiable: GPS can be spoofed. Steps can be shaken. Insurers won't pay for unverifiable data.
- No verification layer exists for human physical behavior.
